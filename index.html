FAQ

How long will the tooth jewel last?

Anywhere from 6 months up to 24 months for the Swarovski Crystals, Brilliance Crystals and Shaped Crystals and 6 to 12 Months for the Real Gold Charms & Real Diamonds (Due to the extra Weight on these Specific Charms). With extra care, they may last even longer.


Can I feel the jewel on my tooth?

The jewels are only 0.4mm or 0.9mm thick and 1.8mm to 3mm in diameter. Initially something on your tooth will feel different, but a few days after the application you will get used to the new feeling.


Will the jewel harm my tooth?

No! The jewel is bonded to the enamel like a orthodontic bracket without any drilling or pain. The procedure and removal can be compared to the placement and removal of orthodontic brackets. When it is removed the gem/charm is simply popped off and the tooth will be polished to remove any remaining bonding material.


Does the jewel impede brushing?

No, the presence of the tooth jewelry will not make any difference regarding dental hygiene. However, it is recommended not to use an electric tooth brush.


What if the jewel falls off and I swallow it?

The jewels are tiny glass crystals or gold. They have no sharp edges. It will basically come out "the natural way" the same as it went inside your body.


Can I still have my teeth cleaned by my dentist/hygienist?

Yes, you can and should still have your teeth cleaned by a professional. Tooth jewelry will not cause a problem for the hygienist cleaning your teeth. The area underneath the jewel is sealed like a filling and will not let in any bacteria. The hygienist can easily polish on top and around the jewel with a soft point.

What are removable caps?

Removable caps (also known as grills) are a type of tooth jewelry that I began making recently, but have been in style for the past 35,000 years, worn by the rich kings and queens. These are the actual caps that will fit over your existing teeth.


What are the different types of precious metals you offer?

We offer:
• 10kt Gold is the hardest type of gold, however, it tends to tarnish quickly, especially for smokers. Not recommended by dentists.
• 14kt Dental Gold will not tarnish as fast 14kt regular gold and if you don't smoke it may not tarnish at all.
• 18kt Dental Gold is recommended for everyone (including smokers) to order 18kt dental gold, because it rarely tarnishes. This is also the gold recommended by all the dentists.
• 22kt Gold never tarnishes and it is what most dentists use in their practices.
• 14kt White Gold looks similar to silver and tarnishes very slowly.
• 18kt White Gold gives the look of Platinum and rarely tarnishes. • Platinum is the strongest material that our caps are made from. Platinum never tarnishes and it’s recommended by dentists.


Can I design my own custom grill and you make it?

Yes, absolutely. Get creative, schedule an appointment, and get a quote! If you can design it on paper or show us a picture. We can custom design your grill to your uniqueness. Be as detailed as possible. Include the following in your drawing.
      • Diamond Quality (SI1 VS or VVS Diamonds)
      • Type of Gold or Platinum
      • Type of design (Grill or Caps)


How long will it take to get my caps back?

About 10 to 15 business days or sooner. That depends on the complexity of the order and payment method used to place your order. We do not offer rush service. As each customer grill should be handled with care and attention. We do not rush quality.


Can I eat/sleep with my caps on?

I do not recommended eating or sleeping with your caps as it interferes with dental hygeine. I reccomend cleaning your gold teeth as often as you clean your real teeth to maintain good hygeine. Gold teeth should not be treated as dental work, but should be treated just like any other peace of jewelry.


How do I keep my caps clean?

You can use a polishing cloth on the outer portion of your gold teeth. You can gently brush the inside portion with a toothbrush. In addition, you can also drop your gold teeth in mouth wash over night.


How long will my caps last?

Your caps will last for as long as you will take care of them. We have customers that are wearing our custom made gold caps for over 10 years now. It all depends on the individual.

ABOUT NYHC

New York Hardcore Tattoos is a landmark in the heart of the lower east side of New York City. NYHC Tattoo is the brainchild of two of the most influential Lower East Side musicians. Vinnie Stigma of Agnostic Front and Jimmy Gestapo of Murphys Law. This legendary landmark opened in 1999 and has been a staple in the hardcore community for over 10 years.

In early 2010 Jimmy and Vinnie added three new partners to the mix. Lars Frederiksen of the punk band Rancid, along with Albany icons, Mike Valente and Tragedy.

In 2015 Nick Caruso and Mike Maney took over the shop and in 2017 passed the mantle to Mark Harada. Mark is currently laboring to maintain the shop's cult status with Sweety, Miykey, Jeremy Miller and young Dean "curtains" Violante. Plus friends and various guest artist from around the globe.

Close to the F, M and J trains. (Delancey/ Essex St.)
